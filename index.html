What I Do.

I'm a Digital Producer working on softwares, website and mobile applications with Minitheory. That means I'm the closest non-researcher, non-designer, non-developer to the things being made. I make sure completion at a specific level of quality and fitness through communication and expertise.

I sit objectively in between the project, the client, and the team doing the work. I'm the fastest learner in the room. Singapore is my home. I brings a strong sense of purpose to my projects and constantly redefines the meaning of “impossible”. I am connected to some of the best people in the industry.
